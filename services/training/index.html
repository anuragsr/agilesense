TRAINING METHODOLOGIES

Agile Sense Coaches can fulfil your holistic Agile training needs by providing certified and non-certified training.

We support the ICAgile consortium as their series of certifications firstly offer a generic range of Agile certifications and not specific to any method and secondly because they have a wide range of personalized trainings, which caters for most roles in the Agile landscape.

Non-Certified Training

Every company is unique in their specific Agile training needs. Many companies do not have a need for certification but do want specific training to upskill their employees, specifically in large scale adoption and the training budget is limited.

Generic Foundational Generic Advanced (not role specific) Generic Role Based Method
Foundational training aligned to the ICAgile foundational training Generic master class, covering deeper general techniques and practices. (Program Management, Agile Games, Detailed Release Management cycle, etc.) Specialist training in:
  • Agile Testing
  • Devops
  • Product Owner
  • ScrumMaster
  • Portfolio Management
  • Release Management
  • Agile Coaching
 We can provide non-certified training in the following methods:
  • SAFE
  • Kanban
  • Lean
  • Scrum

Certified Training

Certified training is useful for personal career development, Agile consultants and companies with certain formal qualification requirements.

We can provide certified training in the following courses:

  • ICP (Agile Fundamentals)
  • ICAgile Agile Team Fascilitator (ICP ATF)
  • ICAgile Business Value Analysis (ICP BVA)

Whichever route you take, you can be certain that Agile Sense has the expertise to deliver personalized quality training.
